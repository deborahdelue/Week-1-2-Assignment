What AVT Is

Applied Visual Thinking is a set of courses designed to give solopreneurs, managers and teams ready access to the bare bones basics of business communication using words, pictures and diagrams.

All AVT Courses
  • Are divided into short, bite-sized morsels
  • Are based on evidence-based learning models and 40+ years of expertise
  • Prepare you to use your new expertise as soon as you finish each course
